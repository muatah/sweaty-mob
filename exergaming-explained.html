The Sweaty Mob Framework

Exergaming.
Active Gaming.
Fitness Gaming.
One Movement.

Three terms get tossed around like they mean the same thing. They don't. Here's what each one actually means, where they overlap, and why Sweaty Mob brings them all together.

The Definitions

Three Concepts. One Goal: Move More.

Exergaming

Games designed so your body is the controller. The movement is the gameplay. You physically cannot play without exercising.

  • Ring Fit Adventure (Switch)
  • Beat Saber (VR)
  • Just Dance (All Platforms)
  • Supernatural (Meta Quest)
  • Fitness Boxing (Switch)
  • Dance Dance Revolution
  • BoxVR / FitXR
  • Nintendo Wii Sports

Active Gaming

Any game paired with physical activity. The game doesn't require movement, but you build it in. You create the rules and your body does the rest.

  • Pushups every time you lose a life
  • Plank during every loading screen
  • Squats between matches
  • Walking on a treadmill while you play
  • Stretching between rounds
  • Jumping jacks after every loss
  • Wall sit during the match lobby
  • Crunches during cutscenes

Fitness Gaming

Game mechanics like points, streaks, and leaderboards layered onto traditional exercise. The workout is the focus. The game keeps you coming back.

  • Zwift (Cycling + Racing)
  • Peloton Leaderboards
  • Apple Fitness+ Activity Rings
  • Strava Segments and Challenges
  • Wearable Step Competitions
  • Nike Run Club Streak Badges
  • Fitbit Daily Goal Celebrations
  • Samsung Health Group Challenges

Where They Coexist

The Overlap Is Where the Magic Happens

Most people pick one lane. Sweaty Mob operates at the intersection of all three. That's what makes the approach different and why it works for gamers who've never considered themselves "fitness people."

Exergaming + Active Gaming

Play Beat Saber as your main workout, then add pushups between songs. The game gets you moving. The active gaming layer pushes you further.

Active Gaming + Fitness Gaming

Do your loading screen workouts and track them with a wearable. Now your casual reps feed into streaks, stats, and personal bests that keep you accountable.

Exergaming + Fitness Gaming

Ring Fit Adventure already has RPG progression built in. Pair it with Strava or Apple Health and you have gamified fitness inside a fitness game. Levels on levels.

All Three = The Sweaty Mob Approach

Use exergaming titles for structured sessions. Layer in active gaming challenges during any game you play. Track everything with fitness gaming tools. That's the full Sweaty Mob system and it meets you wherever you are.
