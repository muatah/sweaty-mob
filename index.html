Meet Coach Mo

The Real Person Behind the Brand

"I'm not just selling ebooks. I'm building a movement. Every rep, every round, every Mob member matters."

I'm Mo Rider — PE teacher, cycling gamer, and the founder of Sweaty Mob. The name says it all: "sweaty" because real workouts make you sweat, and "mob" because we move as one. I started this because I saw kids and adults glued to screens and thought — what if we made the screen work FOR their health, not against it?

What started as an experiment in my PE classes at Sojourner Truth Montessori School in Washington DC became a full-blown movement. 10 ebooks, 5 certification programs, 8,000+ community members, and a school program proving that gaming and fitness aren't opposites — they're the ultimate combo.

Changing the Game for Kids

Real Impact in Real Classrooms

At Sojourner Truth Montessori School in Washington DC, we're proving that exergaming isn't just for adults. Our PE program integrates gaming with physical education, turning disengaged students into active participants.

  • Engaging students who were previously disengaged from PE
  • Integrating technology students love with physical activity
  • Building teamwork through collaborative gaming workouts
  • Measurable improvements in participation and fitness metrics
  • Creating a model for schools nationwide
