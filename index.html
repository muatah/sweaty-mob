Meet Coach Mo

This Isn't a Faceless Brand. It's Mo.

"I saw kids who loved gaming but hated exercise. Adults glued to screens who couldn't find 15 minutes to move. I thought: what if the screen worked FOR their health, not against it?"

I'm Mo Rider. PE teacher, cyclist, gamer, and the founder of Sweaty Mob. The name is the mission: "sweaty" because real workouts make you sweat, and "mob" because we move together. What started as an experiment in my PE classroom became something much bigger.

Today we've got a full library of exergaming guides, certification programs for educators, a school-based PE program at Sojourner Truth Montessori in DC, and 8,000+ community members who prove every day that gaming and fitness aren't opposites. They're the ultimate combo. And I still answer every message personally.
