Phase 01 Build the Challenge on StepUp
01

Create Your Free StepUp Account

Free

StepUp tracks participant activity automatically across Apple Watch, Garmin, Fitbit, Samsung, Whoop, Oura, and phone-only users — all in one leaderboard.

  • →Go to getmotivated.io (StepUp's web app) or download the StepUp app on iOS/Android
  • →Sign up with your email — select "I'm an organiser" when prompted
  • →Set up your Sweaty Mob brand profile: upload your logo, brand name, and a short bio
getmotivated.io — StepUp web portal
02

Create a New Group for Your Community

Free

Groups are where your audience joins and where all challenges live. Think of it as the Sweaty Mob fitness home base inside StepUp.

  • →Tap Create Group → name it "Sweaty Mob" → set it to Public (so anyone can join via link)
  • →Upload a cover image — use an existing Sweaty Mob brand graphic
  • →Copy the group invite link — this is what you share on TikTok, IG, and YouTube
  • →Note: Groups support up to 1,500 members — unlimited groups allowed
Pro tip: Put the StepUp group invite link in your Linktree so it's always one tap away from every bio.
03

Build Your Challenge — Name, Goal & Duration

Free

Inside your group, create the challenge. StepUp supports steps, distance, active minutes, and calories — pick the metric that suits your challenge theme.

  • →Tap Create Challenge inside your group
  • →Name it with gaming language — e.g. "14-Day Final Boss Challenge" or "The 100K Step Quest"
  • →Set your target: e.g. 100,000 steps over 14 days (about 7,150/day — accessible for most people)
  • →Set start and end dates — give at least 3 days notice before launch so people can join
  • →Enable the leaderboard so participants can see each other's progress — this drives competition
Challenge name ideas: "The Boss Battle Burn" · "Level 14 Unlocked" · "The 10K Daily Grind" · "Final Rep Challenge" · "XP Farm — 14 Days of Movement"
Phase 02 Set Up the Google Form for Gift Claims
04

Build the Completion & Gift Claim Form

$0

Go to forms.google.com and create a new form. This is what completers fill out to claim their merch gift. Keep it short — 5 fields max.

  • →Field 1: Full name (Short answer)
  • →Field 2: Email address (Short answer) — needed to send confirmation
  • →Field 3: Shipping address (Paragraph) — street, city, postcode, country
  • →Field 4: StepUp username or screenshot of your completion badge (File upload or Short answer)
  • →Field 5 (optional): T-shirt size if sending apparel (Multiple choice: XS/S/M/L/XL/2XL)
Form title copy: "You Finished — Claim Your Sweaty Mob Gift!" — Keep the confirmation message warm: "Congrats, you beast. Your gift is on its way. Mo will review all submissions within 48 hours."
05

Turn On Email Notifications for Every Submission

$0

Google Forms can email you instantly every time someone submits. This is your trigger to send the gift — no checking required.

  • →In your form editor, click the three dots (⋮) in the top right → Get email notifications for new responses
  • →Toggle it on — Mo's Gmail will now receive an instant notification per submission
  • →Optionally: in the Responses tab → click the Google Sheets icon to auto-log all submissions into a spreadsheet for easy tracking
Verification step: Before sending a gift, cross-check the submitter's StepUp username against the group leaderboard to confirm they actually completed the target. Takes 30 seconds per person — prevents anyone gaming the system.
Phase 03 Set Up Printful & Dispatch Gifts
06

Create Your Printful Account & Upload Merch Designs

Free to set up

Printful prints and ships on demand — Mo never holds stock. You only pay when an order is placed. Set this up once and it's ready for every future challenge.

  • →Go to printful.com → Create a free account
  • →Go to Product Templates → Create your first gift item (recommend: Classic Unisex Tee or Sticker Pack)
  • →Upload the Sweaty Mob logo or a challenge-specific design (e.g. "14-Day Final Boss — Finisher")
  • →Save it as a Template — you'll use this every time you place a manual order
  • →Add a payment method to your Printful account so orders go through instantly
printful.com — Free account setup
07

Place a Manual Order for Each Completer

~$12–$35/item

When you receive a verified form submission, log into Printful and place a manual order to the completer's address. Takes about 2 minutes per person.

  • →In Printful dashboard → click Orders → Create Order → Manual Order
  • →Select your saved product template (the challenge finisher tee or sticker pack)
  • →Enter the recipient's name and shipping address from the Google Form response
  • →Select size if applicable → Confirm and pay → Printful prints and ships directly to them
  • →Printful emails the recipient a tracking number automatically — no extra step needed
Cost breakdown per gift sent: Classic Tee ~$13.25 + shipping ~$4.99 = ~$18–$22 total. Sticker pack ~$4.50 + shipping ~$3.99 = ~$8–$9 total. Budget accordingly when deciding what % of completers get merch vs a digital reward.
Phase 04 Launch, Promote & Reward
08

Promote the Challenge Across All Platforms

$0

Run a 3-day countdown before the challenge opens. Make the announcement feel like a game launch, not a fitness programme.

  • →TikTok: "The challenge drops in 3 days. Connect your Apple Watch or Garmin. You vs the leaderboard." — hook video with challenge details overlay
  • →Instagram Stories: Countdown sticker + "Drop your tracker in the comments — Apple Watch or Garmin gang?" to drive engagement
  • →YouTube Community post: Full challenge breakdown with rules, prizes, and the StepUp join link
  • →Facebook Group: Pin the challenge post — include join link, rules, and prize photo
  • →Mid-challenge: post a leaderboard screenshot on Stories every 3–4 days to keep momentum
09

Reward Completers — Tiered Gift System

Mixed cost

Not every completer needs a physical gift — use a tiered reward system to maximise motivation while controlling costs. This also creates three levels of content moments to post.

All Completers
Digital Gift
Sweaty Mob workout PDF + 20% merch discount code. Sent via email. Cost: $0.
Top 3 on Leaderboard
Merch Item
Finisher tee or sticker pack via Printful. Tagged in a Reel. Cost: ~$18–$22/person.
1 Random Completer
1-on-1 Call with Mo
30-min coaching session. Booked via Calendly (free). Cost: Mo's time only.
Content moment: Post a "Challenge Wrap" Reel announcing winners, showing the leaderboard, and tagging top completers. This becomes your best-performing post of the month and the best ad for the next challenge.
10

Repeat, Refine & Scale

Ongoing

After the first challenge, Mo will have real data: how many joined, how many completed, what the drop-off looked like. Use that to make the next one better.

  • →Run challenges every 6–8 weeks — enough gap to create anticipation, close enough to keep momentum
  • →Increase the target slightly each time — participants level up alongside the brand
  • →Collect testimonials and "before/after" stats from completers — use them in the next launch post
  • →Once the community hits 200+ regular participants, consider adding a paid challenge tier ($14.99) for exclusive coaching content during the challenge period
