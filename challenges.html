Phase 01 Get Set Up
01

Download the Free StepUp App

Free

StepUp is the free step challenge app we use for all Sweaty Mob challenges. It tracks your steps automatically and lets you compete with the whole Mob on a live leaderboard.

  • →iPhone: Open the App Store → search “StepUp Pedometer” → tap Get
  • →Android: Open Google Play → search “StepUp Pedometer” → tap Install
  • →Open StepUp → sign in with Google, Apple, or Facebook
  • →Allow StepUp to access Apple Health (iPhone) or Health Connect (Android) when prompted — this is how it reads your steps
No tracker? No problem. StepUp works with just your phone in your pocket. No wearable required — your phone's built-in step sensor does the work.
02

Join the Sweaty Mob Group

Free

The Sweaty Mob group is our home base inside StepUp. Once you join, you'll see every active challenge and the live leaderboard — this is where the competition lives.

  • →Tap the invite link Mo shared on Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, or Linktree
  • →The link opens StepUp directly and drops you into the Sweaty Mob group
  • →If you haven't installed StepUp yet, the link takes you to the app store first — install, sign in, then tap the link again
  • →Once you're in, you'll see the group leaderboard and any active challenges right away
Can't find the link? Check Mo's Linktree (linktr.ee/ridewithmo), his Instagram bio (@ridewithmo), or the pinned post in the Sweaty Mob Facebook group.
03

Connect Your Wearable (Optional)

Free

If you rock an Apple Watch, Fitbit, Garmin, Samsung, Whoop, Oura, or any other tracker — sync it so your steps count automatically. If you're phone-only, skip this step.

  • →In StepUp, go to Settings → Sync Wearable
  • →iPhone: Connect StepUp to Apple Health → then make sure your wearable's app (Garmin Connect, Fitbit, etc.) also syncs to Apple Health
  • →Android: Connect StepUp to Health Connect → then make sure your wearable's app also syncs to Health Connect
  • →Fitbit, Garmin, Samsung: StepUp also supports direct sync — look for your brand in the Sync Wearable menu
Apple Watch users: If your steps aren't showing, check Apple Health → Steps → Data Sources & Access → drag your Apple Watch to the top of the priority list.
Phase 02 Compete
04

Join the Active Challenge

Free

Inside the Sweaty Mob group you'll see the current challenge with a start date, end date, and step goal. Your steps start counting automatically once you're in.

  • →Open the Sweaty Mob group in StepUp
  • →You'll see the challenge leaderboard — daily, weekly, and the custom challenge date range set by Mo
  • →Your steps are tracked and added to the leaderboard automatically — no button to press, no check-ins
  • →The challenge goal (e.g. 100,000 steps over 14 days) is announced by Mo when the challenge launches
Timing tip: Join the group before the challenge start date so your steps count from day one. Mo always announces challenges at least 3 days early.
05

Grind — Crush Steps Every Day

The fun part

This is where it gets real. Walk, run, hike, dance, play — whatever moves you. Every step counts. The leaderboard updates in real time so you always know where you stand.

  • →Open StepUp daily to check your step count and rank on the leaderboard
  • →Use the group chat to talk trash, cheer people on, or post your daily grind
  • →Keep pace with Active Bot (~10K steps/day) and stay ahead of Chill Bot (~2K steps/day) — gamified virtual friends built into StepUp
  • →Send taunts to friends below you and cheers to anyone climbing the ranks
Pro move: Screenshot your leaderboard rank and post it to your IG story. Tag @ridewithmo — Mo reposts the best ones.
06

Climb the Leaderboard

Real-time ranking

The Sweaty Mob group has three leaderboards that update automatically — daily, weekly, and the custom challenge period. Finishing higher means bigger rewards and bragging rights.

  • →Daily leaderboard: Resets at midnight — see who had the biggest day
  • →Weekly leaderboard: Resets every Monday — consistency wins here
  • →Challenge leaderboard: Covers the full challenge period (e.g. 14 days) — this is the one that decides winners
  • →Tap on any member's name to see their detailed stats
Mystery Mode alert: Some challenges hide today's step counts and only show ranks. Full numbers are revealed the next day. Extra suspense, extra motivation.
Phase 03 Finish & Claim
07

Complete the Challenge

You did it

When the challenge end date hits, the leaderboard locks in. If you hit the step goal — congrats, you're a finisher. Mo verifies all completers against the StepUp leaderboard data.

  • →The challenge leaderboard stays visible after the end date so you can see your final rank and total steps
  • →Mo will announce results on Instagram, TikTok, and in the group chat
  • →If you hit the goal — you're eligible for rewards. If you finished top 3 — even bigger rewards are coming
08

Claim Your Reward

Free for completers

After Mo verifies you hit the goal, you'll get a link to the reward claim form. Fill it out and your gift is on the way — takes 60 seconds.

  • →Mo posts the reward claim link (Google Form) in the group chat and on social media after the challenge ends
  • →Fill in your name, email, StepUp username, and shipping address (for merch rewards)
  • →Mo cross-checks your username against the leaderboard — verified completers get their reward within 48 hours

Phase 04 Shoutouts & Merch
09

Every challenge

Mo shouts out every challenger who finishes. Top performers get featured in a “Challenge Wrap” Reel on Instagram and TikTok. The more you show up, the more you get seen.

10

Earned, not bought
